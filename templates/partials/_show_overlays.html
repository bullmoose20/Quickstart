Canvas Toolbox Help

Use these controls to position and manage overlays on the canvas.

View & Navigation
  • Pan: Drag the canvas background without moving overlays.
  • Grid: Show or hide the alignment grid.
  • Snap: Snap overlay movement to the grid size.
  • Grid size: Sets the grid/snap step (5/10/15/25px).
  • Zoom: Use −/+ to zoom; Reset returns to 100%.
  • Expand: Open the canvas full screen.
Selection & Movement
  • Select: Enable multi-select; use Shift/Ctrl to add/remove.
  • Nudge: Move selected overlays by the nudge step.
  • Nudge step: Sets the nudge increment (1px/5px).
  • Reset Pos: Reset selected overlays to their default offsets.
  • Undo/Redo: Step backward/forward through position changes.
Align & Distribute
  • L/C/R: Align selected overlays left/center/right.
  • T/M/B: Align selected overlays top/middle/bottom.
  • DH/DV: Evenly distribute selected overlays horizontally/vertically.
Output
  • Export: Download a PNG of the current canvas.
  • Settings: Jump to the selected overlay’s settings block.
