Next Steps

Now that you have your Kometa Configuration File, you should check if any errors have been flagged. If they have been, go back to the relevant page and make any necessary adjustments.

(ONLY On Local-* installations of Quickstart), skip down to the Build Your Kometa Run Command and feel free to hit the Run Now button to launch Kometa immediately.

We have added some ASCII headers to categorize the configuration, you can choose your Header Style to change how your config looks!

You can also hit the Download Config or Download Redacted Config button and place it in the config folder of your choice.

Thanks for using Quickstart and enjoy your Kometa journey!

Finally, SMASH that Donate button or Sponsor page to help keep the projects going!
